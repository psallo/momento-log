Privacy Policy

We built Momento Log with privacy at its core. Here's exactly what we collect — and what we don't.

Last updated: June 22, 2025

Summary
  • 📱All your memories are stored only on your device. We have no servers, no cloud sync.
  • 🔒No account, no sign-up, no email required. Ever.
  • 🎙️Voice recordings are sent to OpenAI only when you use the AI transcription feature — and only to process your request.
  • 📍Your location is used only on-device to trigger notifications and save memory coordinates.
  • 🚫We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.
01

Overview

Momento Log ("the App") is a location-based memory app developed for iOS. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the App.

Momento Log is designed to work entirely on your device. The App does not require an account, does not sync to a cloud server operated by us, and does not collect personal information for advertising or analytics purposes.

02

03

Location Data

Location is central to how Momento Log works. The App requests the following iOS permissions:

  • When In Use — to show your position on the map and to save coordinates when you create a memory.
  • Always On — to deliver notifications when you enter the geofence radius of a saved memory, even when the App is not actively open. This uses iOS CLRegionMonitoring, which is managed entirely by iOS and does not continuously track or transmit your location.

Your location coordinates are stored locally in the App's database. They are never sent to our servers (we have none) or any third party.

04

Voice Recordings & AI Analysis

The App includes an optional AI-powered voice feature. When you record a voice memo and tap the AI analysis button, the audio is sent to OpenAI's API for transcription and to generate a suggested title and category.

This transmission happens only when you explicitly trigger it — the App does not record or send audio in the background. The audio is processed by OpenAI's servers and is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

If you do not use the AI voice feature and instead type your memories manually, no audio data is ever transmitted.

05

Momento Log uses the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

🤖
OpenAI API
Used for voice transcription and AI title/category suggestions. Audio is sent only when you use the AI analysis feature. OpenAI does not retain audio for training purposes under the API terms. OpenAI Privacy Policy →
🗺️
Google Maps SDK (iOS)
Used to display the in-app map view. Google may receive map tile requests and your device's approximate location to render maps. Google Privacy Policy →
💳

Used to manage in-app subscription status (Free vs. Premium). RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous device identifier and subscription transaction data from Apple. No personally identifiable information is shared. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →
06

Data Retention & Deletion

All memory data (notes, photos, audio files, location coordinates) lives on your device in the App's local database. Deleting a memory from the App permanently removes it from your device. Uninstalling the App removes all associated data.

We have no servers holding your data, so there is nothing to request deletion of from our side.

07

Children's Privacy

Momento Log is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App in a way that raises concerns, please contact us.

Significant changes will also be noted in the App's release notes.
